Damián Betular is a famous Chef, who was born on October 20, 1982 in Argentina. Baker and pastry chef who is known as one of the best pastry chefs in Argentina, particularly for his artistic pastry designs and his legendary macaroons. He is the head chef at Duhau Palace, Park Hyatt Buenos Aires, and the executive pasty chef at Duhau Pâtisserie.
